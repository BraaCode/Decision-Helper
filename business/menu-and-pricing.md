# Qarrar — Menu & Pricing

## Pricing Philosophy

- **Arabic-first value framing:** pricing presented in SAR and USD, billed monthly or annually (annual = 2 months free).
- **Free tier as wedge:** remove friction for solo users; the habit-forming moment is finalizing the first decision.
- **Team tier as revenue engine:** collaboration, audit log, and governance features gated here — this is where the "defensible decision" pitch converts.
- **Enterprise as credibility marker:** even low-volume, it signals seriousness and unlocks procurement-led deals.

## 6. Price Change Policy

- Prices locked for existing customers at signup for 12 months.
- Any increase announced with 60 days' notice; existing annual subscribers unaffected until renewal.
- Free tier features may reduce over time, but never for accounts that signed up before the change.

---

## 7. Refund Policy

- Monthly: refund within 7 days of payment, no questions.
- Annual: prorated refund within 30 days; after 30 days, case-by-case.
- Enterprise: per contract terms (typically non-refundable after onboarding).
